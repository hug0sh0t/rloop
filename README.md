## About

Rloop is an end-to-end platform designed to modernize property service operations by connecting customers, contractors, and business owners through a single, unified workflow.

The platform manages the complete service lifecycle—from customer requests and contractor management to job execution, verification, and operational oversight—creating a more efficient and transparent experience for everyone involved.

## Features

- Customer service requests
- Contractor onboarding and management
- Job lifecycle tracking
- Completion verification workflows
- Owner operations dashboard
- Secure document management
- Scalable operational architecture

## Vision

Rloop is being built as a scalable operating system for property services, focused on simplifying operations while improving the experience for customers and contractors.
